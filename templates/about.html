About

Our Subaquatic Image Enhancement System is a smart web application designed to improve the clarity and quality of underwater images. Due to light absorption and scattering, underwater visuals often appear dull, hazy, and distorted. Our system corrects these issues to reveal clear and vibrant images.

What It Does

We enhance underwater images using advanced techniques such as:

  • Color correction
  • Contrast enhancement
  • Dehazing
  • Noise reduction

This results in sharper, more natural-looking visuals.

Why It Matters

From marine research to underwater photography, our platform helps users extract meaningful insights from subaquatic images—quickly and efficiently.

Our Goal

To make underwater image enhancement simple, fast, and accessible for everyone.
